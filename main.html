About This website:

Welcome to my website discussing the Background, History and Cultural significance of one of the greatest cryptids in the Americas: The Jersey Devil! The mythology of the Jersey Devil is one woven into the beginnings of New Jersey as a state and America as a whole, and it's story is riddled in enigmatic background and even political fallout. What's more, the Devil's influence to the state of New Jersey is largely underappreciated but goes deep into not just the state's culture, but a lot of popular culture surrounding cryptids, horror and the U.S. general.

This website was made as part of my IS117 final class project under professor Mark Chiusano. I wanted this website to be about a subject that I take great interest in and am extremely passionate about, and the Jersey Devil happens to be a cryptid I have done much research for in the past for things like my radio show in the WJTB Radio club at NJIT.
